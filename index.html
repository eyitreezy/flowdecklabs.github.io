Flowlites Academy

Flowlite Academy is the training arm of Flowdeck Labs, built to groom the next wave of tech leaders in Nigeria and beyond. We run practical programs in product design, product management, and no-code development, combining expert mentorship with real projects. Our bootcamps, internships, and flexible courses equip beginners and professionals with job-ready skills and clear career paths. Whether starting out or advancing your expertise, Flowlite Academy gives you the tools, guidance, and community to thrive in today's digital world.
